About Me

I’m a certified Data Analyst with a strong passion for uncovering insights and solving problems through data. My academic background includes a B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from Sri Dev Suman University, which helped me build a solid foundation in analytical thinking and logic.

Currently, I’m pursuing a BCA in Data Analytics from UPES, Dehradun—an exciting step forward that aligns with my deepening interest in computer science, data modeling, and AI-driven technologies.

I love working with SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw data into meaningful visual stories. My focus is on building clean, interactive dashboards and insightful reports that enable businesses to make data-driven decisions. For me, data isn't just numbers—it's a tool to understand behavior, identify patterns, and drive strategic impact.

Originally from Chamoli, Uttarakhand and now based in Dehradun, I’ve always been driven by curiosity and a constant desire to learn. Whether I’m diving into new analytics techniques, exploring advanced SQL functions, or building personal projects to apply my skills—I truly enjoy the learning process.

My ultimate goal is to become a Data Scientist, combining statistical methods, domain knowledge, and technology to solve real-world challenges and create value through data.
